About this Visualization

Structure

The visualization employs the martini glass structure. The first three scenes shows the data
aggregated by quarter, day of week and hour of day. The last scene invites the viewer to
explore the data further. Each of the first three scenes has stacked bar charts that displays
the data for Stolen Vehicles and Burglaries.

Scenes

My visualization of the data guides the viewer through a sequential set of web pages as the
vehicle for the scenes. The landing page allows for either reading about the visualization
which brings the viewer to this page or continue on with the day of week view of the data.
The two scenes in the middle only have links that move the viewer forward. The last scene
which invites the viewer to explore the data allows the viewer to return to the first page.

Annotations

I used annotations on the first three scenes to provide the viewer with some hints about
what parts of the data might be interesting. I chose to locate the annotations near the
data of interest. I also chose a different color, forestgreen, to draw the viewer's
attention to the note.

Parameters

The last scene, which invites data exploration, allows the viewer to choose to display either
the stolen vehicles dataset or the burglaries dataset. I chose to enable the view to do so
by employing a pair of radio buttons. The viewer can also select as a parameter, the quarter
of the year to display by adjusting a slider. I added some explanation to the bottom of the
page to explain this.

Triggers

Once the viewer updates the radio button selections, my visualization recognizes that as a
trigger to update both the day of week and hour of day charts on the data exploration page
I kept the color sequence aligned with the prior scenese. Therefore, if the viewer selects
stolen vehicles, the bar charts are colored orange whereas if the viewer selects burglaries,
the bar charts are colored steelblue. I chose the color scheme to allow for contrast and
they also are Illinois colors. Once the viewer updates the slider bar, my visualization
recognizes that as a trigger to update two charts on the data exploration page to reflect the
corresponding data for the quarter of the 2017 year. I also included a text box to show the
selected quarter.
